Customizations

HEALTH UTILITY

Health is of utmost importance to everyone. We offer various Health Utilities like a pulse monitor, blood pressure meter, pedometer, an ECG, an oximeter (which is quite handy these days) and much more.
Women can track of their periods cycle and get a reminder for their next due date.
The health utility also detects a heart attack, low sugar, fatigue and when a user falls unconscious. In case of such incidents, the watch will immediately contact a nearby hospital and arrange an ambulance.
'TIME' is crucial in such cases.

OS UTILITY

A sleep app allows you to set a bedtime routine by tracking your sleeping trends and gives you personalized goals to achieve and maintain good health.
Leave your phone behind. Use untethered GPS to track your run’s distance (and play your stored music) on your watch.
Thanks to its new design, you can access your information faster, get proactive help from Google Assistant and access smarter health coaching. All with a swipe of your finger.

BUSINESS UTILITY

Business entities can select the office customization which will link all the team members and send notifications or reminders of a meeting.
The 'main employer' can even see the progress of the employees in the form of stats. Users can keep track of their next flight and keep tabs on their deadlines which is quite handy.
The Office customization offers a lot of utilities which makes going to the office fun.
